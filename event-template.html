Discussion Forum and Technology Showcase

Tuesday 25 March 2025

Location: British Motor Museum, Gaydon | Time: 08:30-17:00

MIA EEMS will deliver a visionary Discussion Forum where you can directly question, and meet, high-calibre business and technology leaders from world motorsport and high-performance automotive.

'The efficient use of energy is central to motorsport'

The Race to Net Zero is still uncertain, with a multiplicity of solutions available to explore. Be a part of this world leading event that determines the future development of motorsport technology. Hear new ideas and gather valuable knowledge and market intelligence on the business opportunities that lie within motorsports progression to Net Zero.

Motorsport is leading the way in new technology and innovation and this is the event to see and hear about it first!

A must attend event for 2025!
